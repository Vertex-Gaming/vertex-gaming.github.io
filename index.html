About Vertex Gaming

We design puzzle-driven mobile experiences rooted in networks, constraints, and problem solving.

Mathematics made playable

Vertex Gaming is focused on creating games that take inspiration from mathematical concepts—especially graphs, networks, and logic puzzles—and turn them into intuitive, rewarding play.

Whether the player realizes it or not, each game is built around meaningful structure: adjacency, connectivity, crossings, coverage, and the kinds of elegant constraints that make graph theory so compelling.

What defines our games

  • Mobile-first puzzle design with quick, satisfying gameplay sessions
  • Graph theory concepts translated into accessible mechanics
  • Clean visuals, intuitive rules, and depth that grows over time
  • Games that reward logic, planning, and pattern recognition
